60 Seconds

About this game:

In "60 Seconds," you'll embark on a frantic race for survival for 60 seconds or 1 minute, dodging deadly obstacles and facing challenging bosses.

The ever-changing dubstep music will heighten the game's epicness, while the background lights up and changes color to the rhythm of the music.

Soon, players will be able to create their own "60 Seconds" levels using songs from the library and share them for other players to enjoy.

Do you have the speed and skill required to conquer this exhilarating challenge and create your own levels of fun?
