Bloodletting

About this game:

In the game, you will step into the shoes of Dr. Alisson Frost, an infectious disease specialist, who is on her way to work at a sanatorium.

You will need to investigate the disappearance of patients and confront a terrifying creature spawned by a mutant disease engineered by a secret organization.

Your mission will be to find a cure for the disease and thwart the organization's sinister plans while struggling for survival.

The story unfolds with surprising twists and shocking revelations, culminating in an unexpected turn to protect humanity.

Are you ready to face the chaos and uncover the truth behind this nightmare?
